Instructions

Objective: The goal of the game is to repeat a sequence of colors and sounds that the game presents. The sequence gets longer and more challenging as you progress. How long can you remember and repeat the pattern?

How to Play

Start: Press the "Start" button to begin the game.

Watch and Listen: Pay attention to the sequence of colors and sounds that the game presents. Each color represents a different sound.

Repeat the Sequence: After the sequence is played, use the corresponding buttons (or gestures, if applicable) to repeat the exact sequence. Be careful and accurate!

Correct Sequence: If you successfully repeat the sequence, the game will add another step to the pattern.

Incorrect Sequence: If you make a mistake, the game will signal that the sequence was incorrect. Take note of your mistake and try again.

GOOD LUCK PLAYING THE GAME!
